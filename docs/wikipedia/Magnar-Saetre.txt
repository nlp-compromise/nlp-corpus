Magnar Sætre (12 November 1940 &ndash; 5 December 2002) was a Norwegian politician for the Labour Party.

He was born in Bergen.

He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Rogaland in 1989, and was re-elected on one occasion. He had previously served in the position of deputy representative during the term 1985&ndash;1989.

Sætre was a member of Haugesund city council from 1971 to 1975.
